Mediations: We have certified mediators on staff who are available to aid in resolving situations without getting a lawyer involved. A mediator is sometimes used as part of an "alternative dispute resolution." Instead of a formal courtroom headed by a judge or magistrate, a case can be heard by a mediator who can help lead both sides to a resolution without aid of a court of law. These hearings tend to be less formal but are designed to be confidential. More courts are making parties go through mediators instead of taking their cases to trial. By going through a mediator this saves money and time.
